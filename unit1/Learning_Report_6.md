## Counterfeit Connection

Emotional & Sexual connection is meant for person to person interaction. I remember hearing this idea for the first time and the spirit confirmed to me how big of a problem this could become. The potential for visual, auditory, and customized pornography is truly a unprecedented peril. But I love these words of advice from Elder Bednar:


Bednars transparency is obvious and deliberate, AI has no feelings, thoughts, or soul. It cannot.

## Work

The section about agency was cool, but the section of the devotional that talked about work resonated with me a bit more. Our capacity to work is a Celestial attribute. The ease of using AI can be a curse that damns our progress by making us unwilling to put in the necessary work that helps us progress and become like God. God and Jesus Christ work really hard (which is the biggest understatement I think I can make) and that is a divine attribute that we are intended to hone here in this world.

I think this part is summed up very nice with the idea of selling birthright for pottage. Bednar emplores us to not sell the exerience of learning & growth.
